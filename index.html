"8/10 rating by viewers"

Hayao Mizayaki, one of the greatest animation directors of Japan has yet again enchanted us with his new film "Ponyo".On June 28th of 2009, the English version of the animated film was debuted in the US at the LA Film Festival.

The story of this adventure follows a five year-old boy who develops a relationship with Ponyo, a goldfish princess who longs to become a human after falling in love with him.
